Step 8 — Bonds & Background Keystones

Level: — Heart: — Bond Rank Cap: — Current Bonds: —

At character creation, you can start with up to Heart Rank 1 bonds. Each bond gets one Bond Keystone. You also get 2 Background Keystones.

Bonds

Bonds represent spiritual connections to other characters. Each bond row includes the bond target, its rank, and one Bond Keystone.

Background Keystones

Add 2 keystones that describe your background, history, or personality.

Background Keystones should not describe your powers. They should be clear, useful, and easy to invoke or compel.
